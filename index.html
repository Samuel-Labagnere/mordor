Mordor

by

Samuel Labagnere

Deep in the heart of Mordor, the great Tower of Barad Dûr rises, its shadow stretching far across the land. Rumors whisper that the Eye has begun to stir, though none can say for certain. This experience invites you to journey into the realm of Sauron’s dark dominion.

Built with the power of Three.js and handmade shaders. Rain falls relentlessly and thunder rumbles in the distance. But beware, for the Eye waits... watching. When it opens, there is no escaping.

This project is an experimental exploration into interactive storytelling through 3D visualization and immersive audio. Though light in design, every detail of the tower and its surroundings contributes to the foreboding atmosphere of this forsaken land.
